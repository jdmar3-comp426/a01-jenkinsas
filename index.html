Pandora

Pandora by John William Waterhouse
Image Sources: Sources

Pandora was the first mortal woman in Greek Mythology that created by Hephaestus under the instruction of Zeus. She was created to punish Prometheus for stealing fire from the gods and giving the gift of fire to the humans. Before she was sent to live among the humans, she was endowed with gifts from all of the Olympian gods.
