The Story of Pandora's Box

Opened up a Pandora's Box by Frederick Stuart Church and Master Z.B.M., Pandora Opening Her Box, 1557
Image Sources: Sources

Among the many gifts given to Pandora by the Olympian gods, she was given a box (more like a jar) that many now know as Pandora's Box. This box was full with all of the evils and diseases of humanity. After Pandora was created and gifted with her many gifts, she given to Epimetheus, Prometheus' brother, for marriage by Zeus. Epimetheus accepted the "gift" of Pandora despite warnings from his brother Prometheus to never take a gift from Zeus.

Once Pandora and Epimetheus were married and she was living among the humans, she got very curious about what was in the box given to her. One day she decided to open it and when she did, all of the evils and diseases of humanity were released into the world. Pandora was stunned by what was happening and tried to quickly close the box to stop all of these things from being released into the world. She only managed to keep Hope inside the box.
